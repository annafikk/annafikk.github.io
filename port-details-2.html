Imbang

"Imbang: Task Management and To-Do List Tracker" is an Android-based mobile application designed to assist individuals, particularly students, in managing their activities and tasks. It comes equipped with a tracker to monitor the progress of each task. Additionally, the application features reminders to alert users when they have been excessively working on a task, which could lead to high workloads and stress.
